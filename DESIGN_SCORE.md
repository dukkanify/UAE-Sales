# UAE Sales — Design Score

**Assessment date:** July 2026  
**Target:** 10 / 10

## Overall Score: 8.7 / 10

The platform now presents a cohesive, premium, handcrafted visual identity. It would be competitive at a top-tier startup demo day. It is not yet at international design award level due to incomplete feature pages and a few remaining polish gaps.
