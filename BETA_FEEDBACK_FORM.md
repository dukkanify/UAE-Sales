## Core Questions

### 1. Was registration easy?

- [ ] Yes, straightforward
- [ ] Mostly easy, minor friction
- [ ] Difficult
- **Notes:** What step was unclear?

### 2. Was adding a listing easy?

- [ ] Yes
- [ ] Mostly easy
- [ ] Difficult
- **Notes:** Which category did you test? Were dynamic fields clear?

### 3. Did listing details look correct?

- [ ] Yes — specs, price, images, location all accurate
- [ ] Mostly correct
- [ ] No — something was wrong or missing
- **Notes:** Did you see any fake or empty spec fields?

### 4. Did chat work?

- [ ] Yes — opened conversation and sent messages
- [ ] Partially — opened but had issues
- [ ] No — could not start chat
- **Notes:** Did you test demo listing, local listing, or own-listing block?

### 5. Was search useful?

- [ ] Yes — found what I expected
- [ ] Partially — results were incomplete or irrelevant
- [ ] No — search did not help
- **Notes:** What query and filters did you use?

### 6. Did the app feel trustworthy?

- [ ] Yes — professional, clear, safe
- [ ] Somewhat — good but needs polish
- [ ] No — felt confusing or unreliable
- **Notes:** What increased or decreased trust?

### 7. What confused you?

Free text. Describe any moment where you were unsure what to do next, what a label meant, or why something did not work.

### 8. What feature is most important next?

Free text. Rank or describe the single highest-priority missing capability (e.g. real checkout, order tracking, admin moderation, notifications).

---

## Optional Deep-Dive

- **Edit listing:** Did editing preserve your images and replace old spec values?
- **Wallet:** Did the balance match your expectations after login?
- **Checkout:** Did "Buy now" show the correct listing name?
- **RTL / Arabic:** Any layout or text issues?
- **Performance:** Any freezing, slow pages, or broken images?
